Our Purpose

People go to sleep everyday without eating anything. We thought about what we could do to influence such a prevalent problem. As a group we felt that while people focus on the broad topic of world hunger, people often overlook those who need help around them. Believe it or not, several people around us do need assistance. We realized we can change the world, if we start small. It has happened all throughout history. Random acts of kindness can make a difference regardless of how simple or grand that act may appear. World hunger is a broad topic and hard to change on a global scope. Our purpose is to show how people can start small to benefit those around them. Our team of four is very passionate and excited to influence such a major issue in society. We want to get involved and hope that you will be interested in this cause as well. Rather than talking about the world we will start small with our communities and aspire to create change together.
