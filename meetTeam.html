The Process

As a team we all worked on different tasks, but never maintained a set position throughout. Our team worked really hard to incorporate all our ideas and overall we worked well together. It was a very interesting project and we were excited to create a final product.

Brianna Mera

I volunteer a lot in my town and I love the feeling of helping others. I cannot begin to express how thankful I am for everything I have. Our project has the potential to give others access to the materials they need to have the same experience. World hunger is a problem, but we need look in our counties first.

Mariam Dardir

I was driven to work on a project like this after watching news airing consequences of world hunger in other countries. Many people all around the world lack food that provide necessary nutrition, and the issue of world hunger is so prevalent yet not publicized. I wanted to provide a platform that could help raise awareness regarding this issue in my community, one county at a time.

Alice Chen

I liked the idea of helping people in need and getting others involved. Raising awareness for a topic like hunger is so important; we need to start working towards a real change. This project allows me to provide a website for people to become informed and slowly begin a necessary process with my team.
